Wood-Therapy

Wood therapy targets fat, muscle, and cellulite, stimulating lymphatic drainage to remove toxins.

Wood Therapy is a natural, non-invasive body contouring technique that efficiently sculpts and smooths the body without damaging the skin or tissue. Utilizing various wooden instruments, each designed for a specific area, Wood Therapy targets and breaks down fat and cellulite while stimulating the lymphatic system to eliminate toxins. This treatment leaves the skin toned, firm, and silky smooth.

How it Works

  • Targeted Body Contouring: Repetitive movements with over a dozen wooden tools are used to manipulate muscles, fat, and cellulite.
  • Lymphatic Drainage: The treatment stimulates the lymphatic system, aiding in the removal of water, fat, and toxins from the body.
  • Fat Breakdown: Enhances the breakdown of stubborn fat and fibrous cellulite for natural elimination.

Benefits & Results

  • Activates the lymphatic system
  • Reduces water retention and eliminates toxins
  • Boosts metabolism
  • Breaks down and reduces cellulite
  • Defines and tones arms and legs
  • Firms and tightens the skin
