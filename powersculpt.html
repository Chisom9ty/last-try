3D-Powersculpt

20,000 crunches, squats, curls, or leg extensions in 30 minutes

3D Powersculpt is an advanced method for body shaping and muscle toning. In just 30 minutes, it delivers the equivalent of 20,000 crunches, squats, curls, or leg extensions. This cutting-edge technology offers exceptional results, setting a new standard in muscle toning and body contouring.

Procedures

3D Powersculpt uses electromagnetic fields to induce muscle contractions similar to exercise. This stimulates energy production in muscles, leading to increased muscle volume and density, improved metabolic rate, and reduced inflammation.

What areas do they target?

3D Powersculpt effectively targets the abdomen, glutes, hamstrings, quads, and arms. It enhances muscle tone, strength, and volume, reduces fat, boosts metabolism, and improves circulation.

FAQ

Recovery varies by individual but generally involves minimal downtime. Most people resume normal activities within a few days to a week. Follow post-procedure instructions for best results.

Possible side effects include muscle soreness, redness, swelling, bruising, or fatigue. These are typically mild and temporary.

Before Care

  • Consultation: Discuss goals and medical history.
  • Hydration: Drink plenty of water.
  • Avoid Sun Exposure: Minimize sun exposure and tanning
  • Medication: Inform about any medications.
  • Shaving: If needed, shave the treatment area a day before.

After care

  • Follow Instructions: Adhere to post-treatment guidelines
  • Hydration: Continue drinking water.
  • Gentle Skincare: Use mild products, avoid exfoliation.
  • Avoid Sun Exposure: Use sunscreen.
  • Compression Garments: Wear as directed.
  • Follow-Up: Attend follow-up appointments and be patient with results.
