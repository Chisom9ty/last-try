3D ultimatepro

Advanced treatment freezes and eliminates stubborn fat cells for lasting, visible results

Experience the power of advanced body contouring with our 3D Ultimate Pro Fat Freezing treatment. This non-invasive procedure uses cutting-edge cryolipolysis technology to target and eliminate stubborn fat cells, delivering a more sculpted and toned physique.

How it Works

The 3D Ultimate Pro Fat Freezing treatment employs controlled cooling to freeze and crystallize fat cells without affecting the surrounding tissues. As the fat cells are exposed to this precise temperature, they undergo apoptosis (natural cell death) and are gradually eliminated by the body’s lymphatic system. This process results in a noticeable reduction in fat in the treated areas.

What areas do they target?

  • Abdomen
  • Flanks (love handles)
  • Thighs
  • Arms
  • Back
  • Bra bulge

What are the benefits?

  • Targeted Fat Reduction: Effectively targets and reduces localized fat deposits in areas resistant to diet and exercise.
  • Non-Invasive: No needles, no surgery—just a comfortable, non-surgical fat reduction procedure.
  • Minimal Downtime: Return to your daily activities immediately after treatment with no significant recovery period.
  • Permanent Results: Eliminated fat cells are gone for good, leading to long-lasting body contouring improvements.

FAQ

Perfect for individuals who are close to their ideal body weight but struggle with stubborn fat pockets in areas like the abdomen, flanks, thighs, or arms. It’s also suitable for those seeking a non-surgical alternative to traditional liposuction.

During the session, a cooling applicator is placed on the targeted area. You may experience an initial sensation of cold, which quickly diminishes as the area becomes numb. The treatment typically lasts between 60 to 90 minutes, depending on the size of the area being treated.

Before Care

  • Hydrate well and avoid anti-inflammatory medications that may interfere with the body's response to fat freezing.

After care

  • Resume normal activities immediately. You may experience mild redness, swelling, or tingling in the treated area, which usually resolves within a few days.
